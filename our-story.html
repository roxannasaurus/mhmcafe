Our Story

Our dream of opening this cafe all started when my sister and I were just kids watching Nana Mhm bake pastries in her small country kitchen. She always put so much care into each step of the process and taught us the value of hard work. Her treats were not only delicious but came from the heart. And its that kind of love we want to bring to you, our customer, every time you stop in for a bite to eat or just a sip of coffee.

At Mhm Cafe, we want to bring you an experience. Good food is in our blood and we want to invite you into our kitchen just like Nana used to and make you feel right at home. From our family to yours lets have a mhm good meal.
